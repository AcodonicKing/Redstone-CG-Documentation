Known Bugs

Description

This page lists bugs discovered during testing of the mod. Bugs are categorized by severity and activity status:

  • High: Dangerous bugs that may severely impact gameplay.
  • Medium: Bugs that cause noticeable issues but are less critical.
  • Low: Harmless or cosmetic bugs.
  • Active: Bugs that are still active.
  • Inactive: Bugs that are confirmed to no longer be active.
  • Assumed Inactive: Bugs that might still be active but lack concrete causes or confirmation.
