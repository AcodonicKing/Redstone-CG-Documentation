Wrong Turn Ceiling RedCu

Severity:

Status:

Code:

Description: When using L-shaped or T-shaped RedCu Wires or RedCu Transition attached to the ceiling, the signal may propagate in the wrong direction.

Cause: The issue was caused by an uncorrected directional case in: net.acodonic_king.redstonecg.procedures.BlockTransformUtils#getLocalDirectionFromWorld. Specifically, when the world direction is North or South, the resulting primary direction must be inverted. When the direction is East or West, the direction must remain unchanged. The missing correction resulted in incorrect local mapping for ceiling-attached wiring.

Consequences: Affected RedCu Wires and RedCu Transitions attached to ceilings may send signals the wrong way, leading to incorrect circuit behavior.

Safe Configurations: Avoid bends in ceiling-attached RedCu Wires and RedCu Transitions.

Solution: This issue is fixed starting with RedstoneCG 0.2.3. Update to the latest version to ensure correct signal propagation.
