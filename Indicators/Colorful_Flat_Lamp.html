Overview

Colorful Flat Lamp Indicator is an indicator block that works similarly to Colorful Lamp, but uses the Flat Lamp form factor with visible Pin Marks.

The indicator changes its color depending on the incoming Redstone signal strength.

For the full description of the signal-to-color mapping and configuration, see Colorful Lamp.

Configuration

The inputs (Pin Mark A) can be configured to face any side by right-clicking. When multiple inputs are connected, the Indicator displays the greatest Redstone signal from its inputs.

Every 16th right-click or a right-click with Colorful Flat Lamp Indicator item toggles base reading state, where the indicator reads Redstone signal from its base, shown by the pinmarks in the corners.
